More About Our Algorithm

We used a Machine Learning algorithm to filter through a dataset containing stocks and values correlating to their respective risks and levels of environmental friendliness. Users are able to enter in a company, risk rating, and stock sector for our algorithm to traverse this dataset looking for stocks meeting the conditions that the user specifies, althewhile pushing the most environmentally-friendly companies to the front.
